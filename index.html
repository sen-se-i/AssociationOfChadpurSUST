About the Association

Rooted in Chandpur,
Thriving at SUST

The Chandpur Students' Association at Shahjalal University of Science and Technology (SUST), Sylhet, is a vibrant community of students hailing from the culturally rich district of Chandpur, Bangladesh. Founded to foster solidarity and belonging, we welcome every Chandpur student who walks through SUST's gates.

Far from home, we become each other's family — sharing stories of the Meghna and Dakatia rivers, the taste of fresh Hilsha, and the warmth of Chandpur's festivals. We bridge homesickness with brotherhood, turning a university corridor into a piece of Chandpur.

Beyond bonding, we represent Chandpur's voice across SUST's diverse association landscape — collaborating, contributing, and celebrating our heritage with pride.

The Land We Come From

The Rich Legacy of Chandpur

Chandpur is no ordinary district. Nestled at the confluence of the mighty Meghna, Dakatia and Dhanagoda rivers, it is a land of extraordinary natural beauty, deep cultural heritage, and the crown jewel of Bangladesh's fisheries — the Ilish (Hilsha).

🐟

Land of Ilish

Chandpur is the Ilish (Hilsha) capital of Bangladesh. The Meghna river yields the finest Hilsha in the world — a fish so celebrated it's woven into Bengali identity and culture.

🌊

Great Rivers

Bounded by Meghna, Dakatia, and Dhanagoda rivers, Chandpur's landscape is shaped by water — a flowing, breathing geography that gives life, trade, and poetry to the district.

🎭

Vibrant Culture

From Baul music to riverine folklore, Chandpur's culture is a tapestry of traditions — jari gaan, shari gaan, and local festivals that echo centuries of Bengali heritage.

🏛️

Historical Richness

Home to ancient mosques, temples, and colonial-era landmarks, Chandpur carries deep historical weight — from the liberation war to pre-Mughal architecture.

Our Mission

Why We Exist

Every Chandpur student at SUST deserves a home away from home. We exist to build that home — strong, warm, and unbreakable.

01

Connect & Build Community

We connect Chandpur students across all departments and batches, creating a tight-knit network that transcends academic boundaries.

02

Assert Our Cultural Identity

We celebrate Chandpur's unique culture — from Hilsha festivals to river heritage events — ensuring our identity shines within SUST's multicultural campus.

03

Support & Mentor

Senior members guide freshmen through academic and social challenges. Every newcomer from Chandpur gets a mentor, a friend, and a direction.

04

Represent at SUST

We engage with other district associations and student unions — making Chandpur's presence felt, respected, and valued across the university.
